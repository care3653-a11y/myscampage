You're safe here

Being scammed is stressful and isolating. Our trained volunteers are here to listen and help you find your footing.

How our volunteers help

MyScamShield is run by trained volunteers. Here's exactly what that means — and what it doesn't:

What we do

  • Listen without judgment
  • Help you understand what likely happened
  • Explain your options step by step
  • Guide you to the right police, bank, and reporting channels
  • Help you prepare the information you'll need to report
  • Point you to resources for financial and emotional recovery

What we don't do

  • We are not law enforcement
  • We cannot recover or refund your money
  • We will never ask you for payment
  • We won't ask for passwords or one-time codes
  • We won't guarantee any outcome

“Reaching out is a brave first step. You don't have to figure this out alone.”

— The MyScamShield volunteer team

Anyone contacting you promising to recover your funds for an upfront fee is running a follow-up scam. Genuine help never costs you money.
